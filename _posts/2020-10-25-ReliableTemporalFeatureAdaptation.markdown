In Correlation Filter-based trackering, unreliable features lead to erroneous target localization and result in tracking failures. To handle this problem, we propose a method for online adaptation of feature weights. We design our learning objective as a convex optimization problem for robust learning of feature weights. We also propose an algorithm to efficiently solve the resulting optimization problem, without hindering tracking speed.
